## INGREDIENTS

Hot Chocolate Base:
1 cup milk
1/4 cup cream
2 tbsp cocoa powder
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup dark chocolate chips 
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 cups crushed ice 
Chantilly Cream (Lightly Whipped Cream):
1 cup cream
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 tbsp shaved dark chocolate to garnish
